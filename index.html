My Experience with Kiwi.com

Back in 2017, I booked with Kiwi.com for a quick getaway in Europe. Being indecisive, my destination choices changed frequently, from Nimes to Nice, Murcia to Barcelona, and even Lisbon and Faro. When searching for flights, I often use Skyscanner, as they tend to offer the lowest prices, sometimes even cheaper than Kayak.com.

I wanted a continuous journey, flying from each destination to the next. Using Skyscanner's multi-stop search, I found what seemed like the cheapest direct flights for my selected dates. The total price appeared normal, maybe slightly above average for the route. However, my experience booking through Kiwi.com was not without its issues, which I'll detail further in this review.

Overall Impressions

Kiwi.com seems to offer a mixed bag of experiences. While some users like Daniel have been pleasantly surprised by their customer support and app functionality, it's important to be aware of potential hidden fees during the booking process.

Based on user reviews, Kiwi.com could be a viable option if you prioritize a functional app and responsive customer service. However, always double-check for any hidden fees to ensure transparency in pricing.

The verdict? Kiwi.com might be worth considering for your flight booking needs, especially if you value real-time updates and customer support. Just be sure to scrutinize the total cost before finalizing your booking.
